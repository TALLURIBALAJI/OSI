About OSINT Framework Portal

Professional open-source intelligence gathering platform

Project Goal

The OSINT Framework Portal uses free APIs and public databases to fetch publicly available data for ethical investigators, security researchers, and HR professionals to validate identities and conduct due diligence research.

Ethical Use Policy

This tool is designed for legitimate investigative purposes only. We strictly use publicly available information and respect privacy laws. Users must comply with all applicable laws and regulations in their jurisdiction.

Data Sources

Our platform aggregates information from public databases, breach notification services, social media APIs, domain registration records, and other publicly accessible sources. No private or protected data is accessed.

Who Can Use This

  • Security Researchers
  • HR Professionals
  • Private Investigators
  • Law Enforcement (with proper authorization)
  • Cybersecurity Analysts

Important Disclaimer

This tool is provided for educational and legitimate investigative purposes only. Users are responsible for ensuring their use complies with all applicable laws and regulations. The creators of this tool are not responsible for misuse or illegal activities conducted with this platform.
